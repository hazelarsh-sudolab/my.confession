Dear Nabilah,

For the past year, I’ve hidden my heart and emotions from you, tangled in circumstances I couldn’t control. Like the ticking blade of a clock counting every second of the day, I’ve struggled with these feelings, resenting how deeply they’ve rooted in me. I never believed my love could be understood or accepted by you.

True love rarely touches my heart but when it does, it shatters me completely. I don’t ask for your love in return. Instead, I hope your heart stays fixed on the one you truly admire, my dear friend, Afif Azfar.

I once dreamed of asking you to walk through life beside me… until I realized I’m not the one you see. I’m sorry for keeping this secret so long. I’ve watched your reposts, your silent struggles, the things you couldn’t voice, just to feel close to you. Now, I confess: I’ve loved you unconditionally.

My tears fell because I, a foolish knight, tried to compete with a prince who already holds your heart. But even in this pain, you remain irreplaceable. If fate ever allowed us to be together, I’d give everything my life, my endless waiting just for you. But since your heart belongs to him, I hope he loves you the way you deserve.

Thank you for changing me, even slightly. The way I see love, the way I understand a woman’s heart… it’s all because of you.


Sincerely,
